ADDITIONALS

NETWORK

To prioritize games, the device has the famous Killer Ethernet E2600 and Intel wi-fi 6 AX201, with this technology gamers gain efficiency online by taking control of the network prioritizing speed at the time of the game.

REFRIGERATION

Acer CoolBoost technology increases fan speed by 10% and CPU/GPU cooling by 9%. Enough to lower the temperature during the heaviest games.

PERIPHERALS

This model is suitable for use with all kinds of accessories: USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-C, 2 USB 3.2 Gen 1, USB 3.2 Gen 2, HDMI 2.0 with HDCP support, Headphone/Speaker/Output jack, ethernet (RJ-45), DC to AC input adapter.

MANAGEMENT

The specific NitroSenso interface, accessed by a key, allows the visualization of the temperature of components, loading speed and fan efficiency, among other important functions. That way, you'll know how your laptop is doing during games.
